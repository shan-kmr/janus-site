where ≠ how

Where you drive is not how you drive.

WHERE / WHEN (ours)

Mileage, time of day, route type, anchors, consistency, highway-vs-local exposure.

Validated: past exposure ranks future path hazard on 1,862 real NYC trajectories (leave-future-out).

HOW (yours / your vendor’s)

Braking, acceleration, cornering, phone use. Bring your own events — the term composes; it is never required.

We derive the first family from any consented source. We compose with the second. We replace neither.
